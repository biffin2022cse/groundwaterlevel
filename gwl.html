Sustainability Instructions

Here are some tips to help you contribute to groundwater sustainability:

  • Reduce water usage by fixing leaks and using water-saving fixtures.
  • Avoid excessive use of fertilizers and pesticides that can leach into groundwater.
  • Support and participate in local groundwater monitoring programs.
  • Promote and practice sustainable land use to protect groundwater resources.
